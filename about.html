Our History & Legacy

Founded in 1972, Horizon Education Institute began as a small college with just 200 students and a vision to provide accessible, high-quality education. Today, we stand as a leading institution with over 15,000 students across our campuses.

Our journey has been marked by innovation in teaching methodologies, groundbreaking research, and a commitment to student success. We've consistently ranked among the top educational institutions nationally for our academic programs and research output.

What sets us apart is our student-centered approach, blending rigorous academics with practical, real-world applications. Our alumni network of over 50,000 professionals stands testament to the transformative power of a Horizon education.
